      <h1>Fair Value Gap <span class="mono" style="font-size:22px; color:var(--ink-faint);">(FVG)</span></h1>
      <div class="def-box">
        <div class="db-label">Definition</div>
        <p>A three-candle imbalance: the wicks of candles 1 and 3 do not overlap, leaving a price zone that wasn't efficiently traded through. Price tends to return to these gaps before continuing.</p>
      </div>
      <p class="lede">In this strategy, the FVG is the <strong>shield</strong>. A swing point inside an FVG hasn't been fully consumed — the imbalance acts as a filter that keeps the level "nested" and worth watching.</p>
