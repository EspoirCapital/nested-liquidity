      <h1>Nested High <span class="mono" style="font-size:22px; color:var(--ink-faint);">(NH)</span></h1>
      <div class="def-box">
        <div class="db-label">Definition</div>
        <p>A swing high resting inside a Fair Value Gap, where price has <strong>not closed a candle body above</strong> the FVG.</p>
      </div>
      <p>Wicks poking through the FVG don't count against it — only a body close invalidates the level. As long as the body close hasn't happened, the NH is still considered live liquidity waiting to be swept.</p>
