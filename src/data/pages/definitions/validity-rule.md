      <h1>The validity rule.</h1>
      <p class="lede">What keeps an NH or NL alive — and what kills it.</p>
      <div class="callout">
        <strong>Rule —</strong> an NH/NL remains valid as long as no candle <strong>closes</strong> (body, not wick) beyond the far side of the FVG that contains it.
      </div>
      <ul>
        <li>A wick piercing through does <strong>not</strong> invalidate the level.</li>
        <li>A body close through it <strong>does</strong> invalidate the level.</li>
      </ul>
