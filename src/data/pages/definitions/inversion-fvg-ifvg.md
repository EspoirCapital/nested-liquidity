      <h1>Inversion Fair Value Gap <span class="mono" style="font-size:22px; color:var(--ink-faint);">(IFVG)</span></h1>
      <div class="def-box">
        <div class="db-label">Definition</div>
        <p>A regular FVG that price inverts — a candle closes its body through the FVG in the opposite direction. What was support becomes resistance. What was resistance becomes support.</p>
      </div>
      <p class="lede">IFVGs are your LTF confirmation after the HTF sweep. Two cases:</p>
      <p><strong>Singular</strong> — one FVG forms. Price closes body through it. Done.</p>
      <p><strong>Multiple consecutive</strong> — FVGs form one after another. Price must close body at the high of the <strong>last</strong> one. If it clears the last one, it clears all of them. This is the priority case — stronger confirmation.</p>
