      <h1>Confirmation candle leaving a large wick on the opposite side.</h1>
      <p>If the candle that inverts the IFVG pushes aggressively in your reversal direction but then pulls back and closes with a long wick against that direction, treat it with caution and avoid entering — it can signal the move isn't holding.</p>
