      <h1>Watch for &ldquo;death candles.&rdquo;</h1>
      <p>On M15/M30/H1, be aware of opposite-direction fair value gaps left behind by a strong candle. Price may tap into these and continue <strong>without</strong> reversing — it's just a small retracement, not your setup.</p>
      <p>Don't mistake this for your reversal.</p>
