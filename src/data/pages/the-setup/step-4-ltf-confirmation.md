      <h1>Drop to LTF for Confirmation</h1>
      <p class="lede">Lower timeframe: M2 or M3.</p>
      <p>Once the sweep occurs, shift down to M2 or M3 and watch for <strong>Inversion Fair Value Gaps (IFVGs)</strong> — see the <a href="#" onclick="event.preventDefault(); return false;" style="color:var(--accent);">IFVG definition</a> for a full breakdown.</p>
      <div class="callout">
        <strong>What "invert" means —</strong> after the sweep, price leaves behind normal FVGs against your anticipated reversal direction. Inversion happens when a candle closes its body through those FVGs in your reversal direction — the FVGs flip polarity and start acting as support/resistance the other way. This body-close-through-and-flip is your confirmation.
      </div>
      <div class="callout warn">
        <strong>Singular:</strong> one FVG — close body through it. <strong>Multiple consecutive:</strong> FVGs one after another — price must close at the high of the <strong>last</strong> one. Clears the last = clears all.
      </div>
