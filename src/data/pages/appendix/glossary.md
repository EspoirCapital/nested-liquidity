      <h1>Glossary.</h1>
      <dl class="glossary">
        <dt>FVG <span>(Fair Value Gap)</span></dt>
        <dd>Three-candle imbalance where the wicks of the surrounding candles do not fully overlap, leaving a gap in price. Acts as a magnet for price to return and fill.</dd>
        <dt>IFVG <span>(Inversion FVG)</span></dt>
        <dd>An FVG that forms after a sweep, inverting the direction of the move. The confirmation signal in the Nested Liquidity setup. Can be singular or grouped (consecutive).</dd>
        <dt>NH <span>(Nested High)</span></dt>
        <dd>A swing high that sits inside a bearish FVG. Requires the FVG to remain intact (no close outside the gap in either direction over time).</dd>
        <dt>NL <span>(Nested Low)</span></dt>
        <dd>A swing low that sits inside a bullish FVG. Same intact requirement as NH.</dd>
        <dt>Sweep</dt>
        <dd>Price breaking past an NH or NL level. Triggers a potential reversal setup if an IFVG forms shortly after.</dd>
        <dt>HTF / LTF</dt>
        <dd>Higher Timeframe (M15–H1) for level identification. Lower Timeframe (M2–M3) for entry confirmation via IFVG.</dd>
        <dt>Intact FVG</dt>
        <dd>An FVG where price has not fully closed outside the gap in either direction since formation. Protects the nested level.</dd>
      </dl>
