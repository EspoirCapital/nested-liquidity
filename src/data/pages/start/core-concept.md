      <h1>Core Concept</h1>
      <p class="lede">Nested Liquidity describes a swing high or swing low that sits inside a Fair Value Gap.</p>
      <p>The FVG acts as a shield around the liquidity — price hasn't cleanly closed through it yet, so the level remains "nested" and untapped. These nested levels are higher-probability liquidity draws because they carry two layers of significance: the liquidity itself, and the imbalance protecting it.</p>
