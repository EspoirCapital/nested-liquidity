      <h1>Where the stop goes.</h1>
      <p>Use a market order for entry.</p>
      <div class="def-box">
        <div class="db-label">Stop Loss Placement</div>
        <p>Place your initial stop loss at the high or low created by the sweep itself — the wick that took out the NH/NL.</p>
      </div>
      <p>Only move your stop loss to half risk once price has moved at least 0.5RR in your favor — don't move it earlier.</p>
      <div class="callout warn">
        <strong>Not breakeven —</strong> price will typically retest the entry before continuing to TP, so moving to full breakeven too early risks getting stopped out on that retest.
